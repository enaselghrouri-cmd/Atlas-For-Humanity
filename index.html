Who We Are

Atlas for Humanity is a non-political, inclusive-to-all Community Interest Company (CIC) dedicated to supporting equity, dignity, and belonging for people navigating change, transition, or challenge.

We work with integrity, empathy, and deep respect for all cultures, identities, and faiths. We believe that confidence and connection grow where people feel heard, valued, and able to keep their identity and heritage close, rather than set aside.

Our approach brings together mental-health informed support, teaching and skill-building, and authentic community connection. We create spaces where learning feels safe, support feels human, and belonging feels possible.

We are:

  • Guided by community voices and lived experience
  • Rooted in trust, partnerships, empathy, and co-creation
  • Focused on learning, capability building, and social equity
  • Committed to helping people grow, take part, and contribute meaningfully to the communities around them

"Atlas for Humanity is not simply a service — it is a community.

Our Commitment

Refugee Support Services

At Atlas for Humanity, we believe that seeking safety is a human right. Our dedicated Refugee Support division works tirelessly to ensure that newcomers to the UK feel welcomed, supported, and empowered to rebuild their lives.

Integration Support

Navigating a new country can be overwhelming. We assist with understanding local systems, housing, and healthcare access.

Language & Culture

Providing English conversation clubs and cultural exchange workshops to break down barriers.

Employment Pathways

CV workshops, interview preparation, and connecting skilled refugees with inclusive employers.

Legal Signposting

Referrals to trusted legal partners for asylum claims and status settlement inquiries.
