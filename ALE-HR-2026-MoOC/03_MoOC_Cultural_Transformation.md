## Change Description

### What will change?
ALE will undergo a cultural transformation to embed the "Accountability Mindset" across the organization. This includes:
- Defining and codifying ALE's culture and values aligned to Accountability Mindset principles
- Engaging senior leadership to model and champion the new cultural expectations
- Developing behavioral frameworks that translate cultural values into observable, measurable actions
- Embedding cultural expectations into performance management, recruitment, and leadership development
- Aligning with Glencore's HSEC and Human Rights Strategy (2026-2028)

### How and when will the change be implemented?
- **By end Q2 2026:** Senior leadership team engaged; company culture and values defined and aligned to Accountability Mindset
- **H2 2026:** Action plan developed with measurements; embedding begins
- **2027-2028:** Continued embedding within teams per HSEC and Human Rights Strategy

*[For Larissa to Complete - Confirm current status vs. Q2 2026 milestone. If behind schedule, provide revised timeline.]*

### What is the objective of the change?
- Shift organizational culture toward personal and collective accountability
- Strengthen safety culture by linking accountability to HSEC outcomes
- Support Glencore's Human Rights Strategy commitments
- Create a foundation for sustainable high performance

### How was the change designed?
*[For Larissa to Complete - Describe design methodology: Was a culture diagnostic conducted? Were employee surveys or focus groups used? Is this based on a specific framework (e.g., Barrett Values, Denison)?]*

### How is the organisation changed and affected?
- All employees are affected - this is an organization-wide behavioral change
- Leaders will have explicit accountability for modeling and embedding new behaviors
- Performance management criteria will incorporate cultural/behavioral expectations
- HR processes (recruitment, onboarding, L&D) will be updated to reflect new values

### How are overall roles, responsibilities, and accountabilities to change?
- **CEO:** Executive sponsor of cultural transformation
- **Senior Leadership Team:** Champions responsible for defining, modeling, and embedding values within their BUs
- **GM HR / Larissa:** Program lead; responsible for action plan, measurement, and integration into HR processes
- **All managers:** Responsible for embedding cultural expectations within their teams
- **All employees:** Expected to adopt and demonstrate Accountability Mindset behaviors

### Risk areas
- **People:** Change fatigue; cynicism if leadership behavior doesn't match stated values; disengagement if poorly communicated
- **HSEC:** Positive intent - cultural change should strengthen safety culture, but poorly managed transition could temporarily distract from HSEC focus
- **Communities:** Accountability Mindset should positively impact community engagement and human rights practices

### Who is involved and why?
All ALE employees across all BUs and terminals. *[For Larissa to Complete - Total headcount]*
