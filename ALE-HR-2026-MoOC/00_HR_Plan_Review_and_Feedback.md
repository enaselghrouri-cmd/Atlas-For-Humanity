## Overall Assessment

Larissa's 2026 HR Plan is ambitious and covers critical areas. Below is structured feedback on each initiative, including observations on what needs strengthening and where Management of Operational Change (MoOC) documentation is required per Glencore's standards.

---

## 1. Organizational Design (MoOC Required)

**Plan Summary:** Review current organizational structure, assess span of control, organizational layers, and budget optimization opportunities.

**Feedback:**
- This is the most significant change initiative and **requires a full MoOC process** per the ALE HR & Compliance TSA (Section 13.1 - Authority to Approve Organisational Change).
- The plan lacks specifics on scope: Which business units/departments are in scope? Is this a single BU or cross-BU impact?
- If the change **materially impacts more than one BU or Department**, it requires CEO recommendation and BoD decision per the authority matrix.
- Even single-BU changes that impact the defined operating model require escalation through GM HR > CFO > CEO > BoD.
- **Headcount variations >5% require CEO sign-off** as per Section 13.1.
- A timeline and phasing plan is missing - when will the review begin and when are changes expected to be implemented?
- **Action:** Larissa must complete a full Change Impact Assessment (MoOC) before any structural changes are implemented. See `01_MoOC_Organizational_Design.md`.

---

## 2. Pay Equity & Risk Mitigation (MoOC May Be Required)

**Plan Summary:** Implement governance structure for pay equity, mitigate labor and reputational risks. Phased approach by department or terminal due to budget constraints.

**Feedback:**
- Good that a phased approach is acknowledged given budget constraints.
- The plan correctly identifies the need for legal advice, action plan, and RemCo approval.
- Per Section 13.3 (Compensation Authority), any changes to salary structures or compensation packages require TSA input and follow the authority matrix up to BoD.
- **Salary adjustments (annual evaluation)** require CEO recommendation and BoD decision.
- **Exceptional salary adjustments** (by individual, excluding EXCO and GM-1) require GM HR recommendation and escalation.
- The phased approach needs clear prioritization criteria - is it by risk level, department size, or cost exposure?
- Missing: Budget estimate for corrections, legal risk quantification, timeline per phase.
- If corrections result in material compensation policy changes, a MoOC is recommended. See `02_MoOC_Pay_Equity.md`.

---

## 3. Cultural Transformation (MoOC Recommended)

**Plan Summary:** By end of Q2 2026, engage senior leadership to define and align culture/values to Accountability Mindset. Develop action plan with measurements. Continues into 2027/28 per HSEC and Human Rights Strategy.

**Feedback:**
- Good alignment with HSEC and Human Rights Strategy timeline (2027/28).
- The Q2 2026 deadline for senior leadership engagement has likely passed - need status update.
- "Accountability Mindset" needs a clear definition document - what behaviors does this translate to?
- Missing: How will culture change be measured? Employee surveys? Behavioral indicators? Incident rates?
- The action plan should include communication strategy, training requirements, and how this embeds into performance management.
- Since this impacts how people work and may change behavioral expectations across all BUs, a MoOC is recommended to capture transition risks. See `03_MoOC_Cultural_Transformation.md`.

---

## 4. HR Governance & Process Excellence Model (MoOC Required)

**Plan Summary:** Implement HR governance and process excellence model ensuring standardization, compliance, digitalization, and efficiency aligned with ALE strategic guidelines and Glencore global standards.

**Feedback:**
- This is critical and directly supports compliance with the TSA authority matrix.
- The plan needs to specify which HR processes are being standardized and what the current gaps are.
- Digitalization component ties to the HR Systems Assessment project (see below) - these should be coordinated.
- If this changes how HR decisions flow through the authority matrix or changes HR roles/responsibilities, a MoOC is required. See `04_MoOC_HR_Governance_Model.md`.

## 5. Supporting Projects - Feedback

### 5c. Performance Management

- Should be tightly linked to the Competency Assessment Framework (mentioned in the plan's KPI for Organizational Design).

## Key Risks Identified

1. **TSA Compliance:** Several initiatives touch areas governed by the authority matrix. Without proper MoOC documentation and approvals, ALE risks non-compliance.
2. **Sequencing:** Some projects are interdependent (Salary Benchmarking must precede Pay Equity; HR Systems must align with HR Governance). A master timeline is needed.
3. **Budget:** Multiple initiatives competing for limited budget. Prioritization framework needed.
4. **Capacity:** Larissa's team capacity to execute all initiatives simultaneously is unclear.
