## Change Description

### What will change?
ALE's current organizational structure will be reviewed and revised. This includes:
- Assessment and potential restructuring of reporting lines across business units
- Review and optimization of span of control (number of direct reports per manager)
- Reduction or consolidation of organizational layers to simplify decision-making
- Budget optimization through headcount alignment and role rationalization
- Implementation of a new organizational model ensuring simplification, clear roles/responsibilities, and strategic alignment

### How and when will the change be implemented?
*[For Larissa to Complete - Provide phased implementation timeline, e.g.:]*
- Phase 1 (Q_ 2026): Diagnostic and current-state assessment
- Phase 2 (Q_ 2026): Design of new organizational model
- Phase 3 (Q_ 2026): Consultation, approval, and communication
- Phase 4 (Q_ 2026/2027): Implementation and transition

### What is the objective of the change?
- Simplify the organizational structure to improve agility and accountability
- Ensure roles and responsibilities are clearly defined and aligned to ALE's strategic direction
- Optimize headcount costs within the SG&A payroll budget (2026 reference)
- Align with Glencore's global standards for organizational governance
